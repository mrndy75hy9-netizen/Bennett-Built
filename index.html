Behind Bennett Built

Meet the Bennetts

Ivan and Nancy are bringing the shop back to life together, with a little more organization, a little more official paperwork, and the same old habit of figuring things out.

Ivan Bennett

Mechanic / Fabrication / Fix-It Brain

Ivan has spent over 20 years fixing things, figuring things out, and getting called over for the kind of problems that start with, “Okay, so don’t laugh, but…”

He’s owned his own repair business before, and opening Bennett Built with his wife is the next chapter: a little more organized, a little more official, and still built around the same thing he’s always been good at: making broken stuff make sense again.

Ivan is the one you want looking at the problem when the obvious answer ain’t answering.

Some jobs need diagnostics. Some need fabrication. Some need common sense. Some need stared at for a minute because whatever broke is clearly being dramatic.

That’s where Ivan does his thing.

He’s practical, stubborn in the useful way, and pretty good at turning “I have no idea what happened” into “okay, here’s what we can do.”

Nancy Bennett

Business Side / Customer Support

Nancy helps keep the business side moving, the details from disappearing, and the whole thing from becoming one giant pile of notes, parts, receipts, and “I swear I wrote that down somewhere.”

With a background in nursing and office management, she’s used to tracking details, communicating clearly, and keeping important things from slipping through the cracks.

Together, they’re building Bennett Built around practical repair, real problem-solving, and just enough organized chaos to keep things interesting.

What we handle

If it makes sense, we fix it. If it doesn’t, we figure out why first.

Guessing is how people waste money. We’re not doing that.

Diagnostics

Lights, no-starts, weird noises, electrical issues, and problems that refuse to act up on command.

Repairs

Brakes, suspension, cooling issues, leaks, worn parts, loose parts, and anything that has gotten loud enough to stop ignoring.

Fabrication & Welding

Brackets, mounts, repairs, custom fixes, and “can we make this work?” projects.

Hydraulics / Pneumatics

Lines, fittings, pressure issues, leaks, motors, and equipment that picked today to act up.

Equipment & Motors

Work gear, small equipment, motors, machines, and things that need to run because sitting there pretty ain’t helping anybody.

Head-Scratchers

The weird ones. The half-fixed ones. The “you just have to see it” ones. We’re used to those.

What we don’t do
